How this page works For you as creator

Update reviews in under 2 minutes

  1. Upload your image to this repo, e.g. inside an images/ folder.
  2. Open index.html, search for <!-- TEMPLATE – COPY FOR NEW REVIEW -->.
  3. Copy that whole block (the <article> ... </article>), paste it below the others.
  4. Replace the src, title, rating, text and the href link.
  5. Commit the changes – GitHub Pages updates your site automatically.
This is a pure static page: no database, no login, no complexity. Just you, your takes, and a clean place to send people before they buy.

Rule: if a new product pops up in your life, it earns a slot here only if you’d genuinely recommend or warn a friend about it.
